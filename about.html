Who We Are

Welcome to Vijay Electronics, your trusted partner in campus automation and security. Based in the heart of Uttar Pradesh, Unnao, we have established ourselves as one of India’s leading manufacturers and suppliers of the revolutionary Automatic School Bell System.

Our journey began with a simple hardware store offering electronics repair in Unnao, but witnessing the struggle of educational institutes with manual time-keeping inspired us to innovate. Today, thousands of schools and colleges rely on our completely automated, digital time management structures to run their campuses efficiently.

At Vijay Electronics, we blend robust hardware with smart digital logic—ranging from basic RTC programmable timers to advanced IoT/WiFi connected smart bells.
