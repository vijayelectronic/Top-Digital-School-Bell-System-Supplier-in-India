Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our school automation systems.

What is an automatic school bell system?

An automatic school bell system is a customizable digital timer machine that triggers your school's bell, hooter, or PA speakers automatically based on a pre-set daily or weekly timetable. It ensures perfect timing accuracy and eliminates manual delays.

Do you supply all over India?

Yes, we are a leading school bell system supplier india. We ship our digital controllers and MP3 bell systems nationwide, including major cities like Delhi, Mumbai, Pune, Bangalore, Hyderabad, and Chennai via reliable courier services.

Can I play prayer songs on the bell system?

Absolutely. Our school voice announcement system supports MP3 files via SD card or USB pendrive. You can program morning prayers, national anthems, and custom Hindi/English voice alerts for different periods.

How does the IoT/WiFi Smart Bell work?

The iot based school bell system connects to your local campus WiFi. You can log into a user-friendly mobile app or web browser dashboard to manage schedules remotely without touching the hardware unit.

Do you offer CCTV Camera installation?

Yes, Vijay Electronics provides complete cctv camera installation in unnao, Kanpur, and Lucknow. We specialize in Dahua, CP Plus, and Hikvision IP camera setups for comprehensive school and home security.

What if there is a power cut? Will the timer reset?

No. All our timer machines come with a built-in Lithium battery backup (RTC memory) specifically for the clock and schedule memory. However, to actually ring the bell during a power cut, an external UPS/Inverter is required for the speakers or hooter.
